Our Approach

We don't just install.
We manage your entire project.

We're a small team of Gas Safe registered engineers who specialise in taking the stress out of renovation projects. Whether it's a complete bathroom transformation, a new heating system, or a full house refurbishment, we manage it from start to finish.

That means coordinating electricians, tilers, plasterers and any other trades needed. You get one point of contact, clear communication, and a project that runs on time.

No chasing multiple contractors. No miscommunication between trades. No headaches.

Frequently Asked Questions

Which areas do you cover?

We work throughout West Yorkshire, including Leeds, Bradford, Huddersfield, Halifax, Wakefield, Harrogate, Holmfirth, Hebden Bridge, Todmorden, Dewsbury, Batley, Keighley and surrounding areas.

Are you Gas Safe registered and insured?

Yes. We're Gas Safe registered with public liability insurance, and all proper certifications are provided as standard.

Do you manage the whole project, including other trades?

Yes. We coordinate electricians, tilers, plasterers and any other trades needed, so you get one point of contact and a project that runs on time. No chasing multiple contractors.

How much will my project cost?

Every project is different, so we come out, discuss what you're looking to do, and give you an honest assessment of costs and timescales. Quotes are free, clear and no-obligation.

How long does a bathroom refit take?

It depends on the size of the bathroom and the work involved. We'll give you an honest timescale with your quote, and because we coordinate all the trades ourselves, the project stays on schedule.

Do you take on smaller plumbing and gas jobs too?

Yes. Alongside full renovations we handle day-to-day repairs, servicing and installations — boiler breakdowns, leaking pipes and gas safety certificates.
